Getting to Know Your Brewery

Big Time is a Classic American Alehouse brewing all our own beer in our on-site microbrewery. Our award winning beer and tasty pub food have been one of Seattle's "U-District" traditions since 1988.Big Time Brewery &Alehouse has been in business in Seattle's colorful "U-District" since 1988. We are a classic American Alehouse located in an old yellow brick building with high ceilings and lots of stained wood. The pub has an antique bar, an extensive bottle and can collection, numerous historical brewery photographs, and lots of old beer signs and trays.

Big Time usually has at least 10 ales on tap, plus on Thursdays and Fridays we feature a cask conditioned "real ale" on the handpump. Our standard brews; Prime Time Pale Ale, Atlas Amber Ale, Coal Creek Porter and Bhagwan's Best IPA are on tap at all times. The other three taps and the cask conditioned pump rotate from week to week with some of the many specialty beers we produce over the year.

The kitchen features pizza, sandwiches (try our incredible fried chicken sandwich), salads, pomme frites with dipping sauces and changing daily specials. We serve generous portions at very reasonable prices. Food is available until 11:00 pm.

The pub is open from 11:30 am to 12:30 am seven days a week and brewery tours are available Monday thru Friday days or by appointment.

The Brewery

Big Time's brewery is a 14 barrel JV Northwest system consisting of a gas fired brew kettle, an infusion mash tun with mixer, a hot liquor back, a wort cooler, (5) 14 barrel fermenters, a diatomaceous earth filter, a bright beer tank, and (12) 7 bbl serving tanks. We sell most of our beer at the pub but do wholesale a limited number of kegs to a few Seattle area taverns. We currently brew approximately 1,500 barrels of beer per year
